The smartest way for designers and recruiters to connect for opportunities

Ta-Da is the world's first job-seeking platform built entirely for designers

Our Product

Ta-Da is built to allow all types of designers to have a platform to best represent themselves in a way that highlight their careers in the best light possible. It combines resumes and portfolio in a seamless way.

Our Vision

Our team is excited to build the next leading job-seeking platform that allows all type of designers to get matched with the right hiring managers and recruiters.

Technology

We use smart tagging system that allow designers to be able to tag every detail of their experience. Those tags will feed into the machine learning technology that will accurately match designers with the right recruiters.
